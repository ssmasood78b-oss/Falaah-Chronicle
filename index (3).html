Our Purpose

What is Falaah?

A free, public resource for Pakistan's social welfare landscape.

F
"Falaah" - prosperity, welfare, flourishing
The Story

Built to make social work visible.

Pakistan is home to thousands of NGOs doing extraordinary work, from the mountains of Gilgit-Baltistan to the coastal communities of Sindh. Yet for most people, finding and connecting with these organisations is difficult and fragmented.

Falaah was created to change that. Developed as a community service initiative in collaboration with Shah Qutub Welfare Foundation (SQWF), it is a free, openly accessible directory that brings Pakistan's social work landscape into one searchable, categorised resource.

The name Falaah comes from Arabic and Urdu, meaning prosperity, welfare and flourishing. A word that sits at the heart of every organisation listed here.

Our Principles

What guides this project

Always Free

Falaah will never charge NGOs to be listed or users to access information. Public benefit is the only purpose.

Verified Data

All information is compiled from publicly available sources. We do not make editorial judgements on any listed organisation.

Inclusive Coverage

We aim to represent all provinces, all causes, and all scales of social work, from national foundations to local community efforts.

Community Driven

Anyone can submit an NGO for listing. The directory grows with the community's knowledge and contributions.

Who We Are

A beacon of hope and compassion.

The Shah Qutub Welfare Foundation (SQWF) is a non-profit organisation dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of Pakistan's most vulnerable. Founded on the principles of service and community, SQWF works to uplift underprivileged individuals through education, healthcare and social support.

With unwavering commitment, SQWF aims to create a brighter future for all, empowering individuals to break the cycle of poverty and adversity through collective effort and philanthropic spirit.

Falaah is one of SQWF's initiatives to strengthen Pakistan's civil society infrastructure, making it easier for citizens to find, support and engage with welfare organisations across the country.

Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

1. About This Portal

Falaah is a free, publicly accessible NGO directory operated by Shah Qutub Welfare Foundation (SQWF). It is provided as a public resource and does not represent, endorse, or verify the work of any listed organisation beyond what is publicly available.

2. Data Sources

All information is compiled from publicly available sources including NGO websites, official publications and public registries. Falaah does not collect or publish any private or confidential information about listed organisations.

3. Accuracy

While we make every effort to ensure accuracy, Falaah cannot guarantee the completeness or currency of all listings. If you find an error, contact us at info@sqwf.org.

4. Privacy Policy

Falaah does not collect personal data from visitors beyond what is voluntarily submitted through our contact or submission forms. Submissions are used solely to update the directory or respond to enquiries. We do not sell or share submitted data.

5. Intellectual Property

NGO names, logos and descriptions remain the intellectual property of their respective organisations. They are displayed solely for identification purposes within this directory.
